## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Kelia ("the App"), a family digital assistant developed by Kelia Apps. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our App.

By using Kelia, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information

To use Kelia, you must create an account. We collect:

- **Name**
- **Email address**
- **Password** (securely encrypted — we never see or store it in plain text)

Your account lets you and your family members securely access your shared family data across devices.

### 2.2 Information You Provide

- **Family member profiles** — names, roles, ages, and birthdays you add manually
- **Events and appointments** — calendar entries you create
- **Shopping lists and tasks** — items you add to lists
- **Home maintenance records** — maintenance tasks, rooms, and notes
- **Documents** — titles, categories, expiry dates, and optionally photos of physical documents you choose to attach
- **Memories** — photos, titles, descriptions, dates, and the family members present, that you add to your family timeline
- **Time Capsules** — photos, videos, voice recordings, and written messages you choose to seal and schedule for future opening
- **Budget entries** — expense descriptions, amounts, and categories you track
- **Family chat messages** — text messages you send to other members of your family within the App

### 2.3 Information Collected Automatically

- **Location data** — we request your device location solely to display local weather. Location is never stored on our servers
- **Device language** — used to display the App in your preferred language
- **Notification permissions** — used to send reminders and briefings if you enable them

### 2.4 Permissions We Request

| Permission | Why we ask for it |
| --- | --- |
| **Camera & Photo Library** | To attach photos to Memories, Documents, and Time Capsules |
| **Microphone** | To record voice messages for Time Capsules |
| **Location** | To show local weather on your Home screen |
| **Notifications** | To send reminders, Morning Briefings, and family chat alerts |

You can grant or revoke any of these permissions at any time in your device settings. The App remains usable if you decline a permission, though the related feature (e.g. attaching a photo) will not work.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide exclusively to:

- Create and secure your account
- Display your family's agenda, lists, budget, and home information
- Let you and invited family members share and view the same household data
- Provide AI-powered assistance through our integrated assistant, using your real family context to give relevant answers
- Show local weather based on your location
- Send you notifications and reminders you have requested

**We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.**

---

## 4. Family Sharing

Kelia is designed for families to organize their life together. When you register, a family group is automatically created for you, along with a unique **invite code**.

- Only people you personally share your invite code with can join your family
- Family members who join can view and contribute to shared calendar events, lists, budget, memories, documents, and family chat
- You can see and share your invite code at any time from the Family screen
- You remain in control of who has access to your invite code — Kelia never shares it on your behalf

---

## 5. Data Storage

Your personal data is stored using **Supabase**, a secure cloud database provider. All data is:

- Transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections
- Protected by database-level security rules ensuring that **only your own family members can ever access your family's data** — no other Kelia user can see it
- Associated with your personal account, secured by your email and password

Photos, videos, and voice recordings you upload (Memories, Documents, Time Capsules) are stored in encrypted cloud storage linked to your account.

## 7. AI Assistant

Kelia includes an AI assistant. When you interact with the assistant:

- The App builds a summary of your relevant family context (e.g. today's events, active reminders) directly from your own account data
- Your message and this context are sent to OpenRouter's servers to generate a response
- This data is processed according to OpenRouter's privacy policy
- We do not permanently store your conversation history on our servers

---

## 8. Location Data

Kelia requests access to your device's location to display local weather. We:

- Only access location when the App is in use
- Never store your location on our servers
- Never share your location with third parties
- You can revoke location permission at any time in your device settings

---

## 9. Push Notifications

If you enable reminders, Morning Briefing, or family chat alerts, Kelia will send push notifications. You can disable these at any time in your device settings or within the App.

---

## 10. Children's Privacy

Kelia is designed as a family app and may be used to manage information about children, added by an adult user as family member profiles. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13 without parental involvement. All child-related data (name, age, birthday) is entered and managed by the adult user of the App who controls the family account.

---

## 11. Data Retention & Account Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can permanently delete your account and associated personal data at any time by going to:

**Settings → Zona pericolosa ("Danger Zone") → Elimina account ("Delete account")**

This action is permanent and cannot be undone. If you simply wish to sign out without deleting your data, use **Disconnetti account ("Log out")** instead.
