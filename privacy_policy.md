# Privacy Policy — Kelia

**Last updated: June 17, 2026**
**Developer: Kelia Apps**

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Kelia ("the App"), a family digital assistant developed by Kelia Apps. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our App.

By using Kelia, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Family member names and profiles** — names, roles, ages, and birthdays you add manually
- **Events and appointments** — calendar entries you create
- **Shopping lists and tasks** — items you add to lists
- **Home maintenance records** — maintenance tasks and notes
- **Documents** — document titles, categories, and expiry dates you enter

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Location data** — we request your device location solely to display local weather. Location is never stored on our servers
- **Device language** — used to display the App in your preferred language
- **Notification permissions** — used to send morning briefings and reminders if you enable them

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide exclusively to:
- Display your family's agenda, lists, and home information
- Provide AI-powered assistance through our integrated assistant
- Show local weather based on your location
- Send you notifications and morning briefings you have requested

**We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.**

---

## 4. Data Storage

All your personal data is stored on a secure server hosted by Railway (railway.app). Data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections.

Your data is associated with your device and is not linked to any user account or personal identifier beyond what you voluntarily provide.

## 6. AI Assistant

Kelia includes an AI assistant powered by OpenRouter. When you interact with the assistant:
- Your messages and family context are sent to OpenRouter's servers to generate responses
- This data is processed according to OpenRouter's privacy policy
- We do not permanently store your conversation history on our servers

---

## 7. Location Data

Kelia requests access to your device's location to display local weather. We:
- Only access location when the App is in use
- Never store your location on our servers
- Never share your location with third parties
- You can revoke location permission at any time in your device settings

---

## 8. Push Notifications

If you enable Morning Briefing or reminders, Kelia will send push notifications. You can disable these at any time in your device settings or within the App.

---

## 9. Children's Privacy

Kelia is designed as a family app and may be used to manage information about children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. Family member profiles are created and managed by the adult user of the App.

---

## 10. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as you use the App. You can delete all your data at any time by going to **Settings → Delete all data** within the App.

---

## 11. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access the personal data stored in the App
- Delete your data at any time through the App settings
- Withdraw consent for location access or notifications via device settings

---

## 12. Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or deletion. All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS.
